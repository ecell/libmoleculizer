Cpt-omni-kinase

This demo shows a use in cpt of the omni-gen reaction generator to simulate a standard kinase-phosphorylating-substrate reaction regulated by phosphorylation of the kinase, X. The simulation parallels that of the "plain" omniKinase demo and is for testing the compartmental version of the omni-gen reaction generator.

The kinase X is introduced at time 1 in unphosphorylated form, and it binds to most of the substrate, but no phosphorylation of the substrate takes place. At time 2, more kinase X is added, but in phosphorylated form. Now, the substrate gets phosphorylated, as the rising red line shows.
