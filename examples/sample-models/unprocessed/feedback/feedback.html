feedback demo

This demo adds two negative feedbacks to the abstract signaling pathway in the "heinrich" demo. The original abstract pathway was the subject of the paper "Mathematical Models of Protein Kinase Signal Transduction," by Heinrich, Neel, and Rapoport. Molecular Cell 9, 957-970,2002.

simulation input

This plot shows the activation of the kinase cascade. In this "feedback" version, active-kin1 and active-kin2 are deactivated by active-kin3, in addition to (constant) deactivation by ptase-x.

This plot shows the constant levels of active-receptor, along with the phosphatase ptase-x. The phosphatase works to dephosphorylate all the activated protein kinases. In this "feedback" version, active-kin3 also degrades active-kin1 and active-kin2.
