mols

There is "room" for many different kinds of mols, since it is expected that many different kinds of indivisible components may form complexes, DNA/genes are expected in particular for the study of transcription factor binding to regulatory regions.

But at this point in Moleculizer's development, the only kind of mol is a "mod-mol," which represents a simple protein that admits user-defined modifications (like phosphorylation or alkylation) at user-specified modification sites.
