cpt-uniMolPtase

This demo checks the compartmental version of the uni-mol-gen reaction generator by setting up a phosphatase/substrate reaction using the uni-mol-gen generator. Here, the generator makes a binary reaction between the phosphatase and its substrate, leaving the phosphatase unchanged. The reaction is, of course, dependent on the substrate's being phosphorylated.

simulation input

The kinase is introduced at time 1, initiating accumulation of the phosphorylated substrate. At time 2 sec., the phosphatase is introduced, causing the amount of phosphorylated substrate to drop off.
