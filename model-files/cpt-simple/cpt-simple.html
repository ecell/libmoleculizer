Cpt-simple

This demo shows a simple dimerization/decomposition reaction pair. There are three compartments, alpha, beta, and gamma, all in a row. All the A reactant starts in compartment alpha, and all the B reactant starts in compartment gamma. Thus, for A and B to dimerize, they must both diffuse out of their compartments. Toward the end of the simulation, the center compartment, beta, still contains more of the AB dimer than the outside compartments.
