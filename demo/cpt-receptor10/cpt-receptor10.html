Compartmental simulations of receptor complex

These compartmental simulations are mainly about seeing/studying the effects of Ste5 dimerization on Ste5 migration to the membrane when the alpha pathway is activated and the diffusive properties of Ste5-dimer-containing molecules in the membrane.

There are more than one simulation; they differ in the circumstances under which Ste5-Ste5 binding can occur:

  • version 1 - two Ste5's can dimerize only when both are bound to Ste4.
  • version 2 - two Ste5's can dimerize when either of them is bound to Ste4.
  • version 3 - a "gradual" cooperativity scheme for Ste5-Ste5 binding.
  • version 4 - same "gradual" cooperativity scheme for Ste5-Ste5 binding as above. Ste5-Ste5 binding decomposition slower and Ste5 diffusion slower, to see Ste5 dimer diffusion in the membrane.
