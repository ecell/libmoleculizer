Moleculizer simulation of receptor complex

This simulation involved 35 species and 103 reactions. There were 5,880,593 reaction events in the simulation.

This plot shows pheromone bound to receptor and the larger pheromone-Ste2-Gpa1 complex that enables nucleotide exchange in Gpa1.

The plots of Ste4-Gpa1-total complexes and Ste4-Gpa1-GDP nearly overlap.

The population of Ste4-Gpa1-GTP complexes rises to a surprisingly high level.

This plot showing all the Gpa1 gradually getting GTP-bound is consistent with plot above showing the gradual, complete loss of Ste4-Gpa1-GDP complex.
