cpt-simple-stoch

This demo checks simple 'stoch-species' by doing the simple compartmental dimerization-decomposition using them.

simulation input

A starts out in the alpha compartment, one of the two 'outside' compartments.

B starts out in the gamma compartment, one of the two 'outside' compartments.

AB tends to form in the beta compartment, the center compartment where A and B meet and largely combine to make AB.
