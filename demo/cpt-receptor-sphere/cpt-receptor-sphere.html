"Spherical" compartmental simulations of receptor complex

These compartmental simulations are mainly about seeing/studying the effects of Ste5 dimerization on Ste5 migration to the membrane when the alpha pathway is activated and the diffusive properties of Ste5-dimer-containing molecules in the membrane.

These simulation differ in the number of compartments and their arrangement:

  • version 1 - 8 membrane compartments, 8 cytoplasm compartments, 1 central compartment.
  • version 2 - 16 membrane compartments, 16 cytoplasm compartments, 1 central compartment.
  • version 3 - same as above, but adding Ste5 oligomerization.
  • version 4 - same as above, but enhancing Ste5 oligomerization.
  • version 5 - mini-cell version, with new diffusion rates.
  • version 6 - mini-cell, not so slow diffusion.
  • version 7 - mini-cell, not so slow diffusion, adjusted binary reaction rates.
